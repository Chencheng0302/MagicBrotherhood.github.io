Magic Brotherhood

起于2013年LOL大火时期的意外组队，随着时间磨合之后，在次年3月26日建队. 随后的几年里，成员数目剧增，又于2015年7月26日建立了 QQ群。时至今日，已有成员约34人。

Arise from an occasional teamwork in 2013, which is one of the hottest period of LOL. Increasingly rapid developing brings considerable scale of members, thus the first official meadia was founded with QQ Group Chat on 26th July 2015. Nowadays, there are approximately 34 members.
